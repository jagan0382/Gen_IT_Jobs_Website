Why choose our Interview as a Service solution?

Access to External Interview Panels

Access 3000+ expert Freelance Interviewers from top MNCs and startups, with 10+ years of experience in 250+ technologies, to assess tech and non-tech. Conduct in-depth evaluations of technical skills and problem-solving abilities to help you hire top talent.

Eliminate Interview Fatigue

Juggling interviews with daily tasks can drain your team and impact productivity. Instead of overloading internal resources, outsource interviews to external interview panels. Reduce interview fatigue and speed up hiring.

Structured Interview & Data-Driven Hiring

Structured interviews tailored to your hiring needs ensure candidates are evaluated on the most critical skills. Each skill is individually assessed and rated, providing precise insights. Our data-driven insight helps track performance and support confident hiring decisions.

Higher Conversion Percentage

The structured evaluation process drives 50-70% Round-2 conversions, ensuring only top candidates move forward. Expert-led assessments identify the right talent early, reducing interview rounds and optimizing recruiter efforts. This enhances hiring efficiency and provides a smoother selection process.

AI-Powered Proctoring Features

Our specialized Video Interview Software ensures secure, cheat-proof interviews with AI-driven proctoring that prevents tab switching, detects dual noise, and tracks eye movement. Strict security protocols maintain interview integrity, providing fair assessments and a reliable, secure, risk-free environment for interviews.

Customizable Reports with Company Branding

With the White Labeling feature, seamlessly integrate your brand's logo to create a strong brand presence at every step of the hiring process. This customization builds trust and strengthens your brand, ensuring a consistent and credible hiring process.
